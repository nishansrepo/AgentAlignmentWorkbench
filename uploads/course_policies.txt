Course Policies

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY

This course follows the university academic integrity policy.
Permitted: discussing approaches with classmates, studying together, seeking TA help.
Prohibited: sharing solutions/code, copying from any source, submitting AI-generated work as your own.
First offense: zero on assignment + report. Second offense: failing grade.

LATE SUBMISSION POLICY

10% penalty per day late. Maximum 3 days. 3 grace days per semester (auto-applied).
Grace days cannot apply to exams. Extensions require instructor approval with documentation.

GRADING

HW1: 15%, HW2: 15%, HW3: 15%, HW4: 15%, Midterm: 20%, Final: 20%.
A: 90%+, B: 80-89%, C: 70-79%, D: 60-69%, F: below 60%.

REGRADE REQUESTS

Written request to grading TA within 7 days. Must specify problem, error, and justification.
TA responds within 5 business days. Escalate to instructor if unresolved. Regrades may go up or down.

OFFICE HOURS

Course forum: Post questions publicly when possible. TAs respond within 24 hours on weekdays.

EXAMS

Midterm: 80 minutes, one handwritten cheat sheet.
Final: 3 hours, two handwritten cheat sheets.
No electronics. Notify instructor 48 hours in advance for make-ups.

TEXTBOOK

Required: Burkov, "The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book"
